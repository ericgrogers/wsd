About The Guitar Shop

Top

Whether you are a professional musician or just a simple bedroom guitar player, the most important thing is to take care of your guitar. Manufacturers exhibit great care in their selection of woods to make the guitar stunning visually, and sonically. No matter if you play acoustic or electric, we can help you keep your guitar in the best shape possible.

At The Guitar Shop, we are all about helping you to make your instrument last a lifetime. No matter the condition of your instrument now, we’ll help you make it better! We provide everything you need to care for your guitars, from “tips and tricks” that every touring musician should know, to sound advice that the even the bedroom guitarist can use. If at any time you feel that your guitar could use professional service, we can assist you in locating the nearest reputable technician.

We will teach your how to change your strings, and prolong their life to keep your instrument sounding the way it should. We will also teach you when and how to clean your guitar properly, prolonging the life of the finish and fret board. Once you have the basics under control, we’ll also show you some “what not to do” situations that you may be overlooking, and how to store your guitar properly when you won’t be playing it for a while.

If at any time you need professional assistance, we will help you locate the nearest reputable shop to get your guitar the care it needs. Regularly scheduled maintenance is important to the overall health of your instrument, and is often better left to a professional.
